// Mission
Building the engineers
of tomorrow — today.
The Robotics Club at IIT Dharwad is a student-run community of builders, coders, and tinkerers. We design and construct machines that push the limits of what's possible — from autonomous ground robots and swarm drones to brain-computer interfaces and precision surgical arms. Every project is hands-on, every member contributes, and every idea is taken seriously.

// What We Do
Build. Break.
Iterate.
We run weekly build sessions, monthly workshops, and semester-long project tracks. Members work across disciplines — mechanical design, embedded firmware, computer vision, and machine learning — to ship real, working systems.
